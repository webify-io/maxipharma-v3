Advancing Health Through Innovation

MaxiPharma Laboratories (Pty) Ltd. is a South African company, providing consulting services in the areas of all type Pharmaceutical dosage from development, and manufacturing. We are also now pleased to offer non-GMP development trial services in our new, small-scale laboratory. Currently we are equipped for direct blending, high shear granulation, fluid bed granulation, tablet compression and encapsulation. We also have induction heat sealing capabilities for hand packaged bottles.
